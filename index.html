About Us

Skylinky Logistics Limited, a Zambian registered company established in 2018, stands as a global leader in freight and logistics services. Our mission is clear: to provide swift, reliable, and cost-effective logistic solutions to both local and international clientele. Employing a customer-centric approach, we prioritize the delivery of quality services ahead of schedule. Our personalized customer service caters to diverse needs, timeframes, and budgets. With operational branches in Sesheke, Kitwe, Ndola, Mazabuka, Katima Mulilo, Kazungula, Nakonde, Chirundu, Chanida, kasumbalesa, Sakania Border Post, and Ndola, alongside our headquarters in Lusaka, we offer a comprehensive range of services through well-structured divisions, including Construction, Transport, Clearing and Forwarding, and more. Renowned for our expertise, we handle exports with utmost professionalism, ensuring maximum productivity and efficiency for both individuals and companies. Skylinky Logistics: delivering tailored logistics solutions on time, every time.
