Clearing

At Skylinky, we pride ourselves on our commitment to efficiency and reliability in clearing consignments at any port of entry in Zambia. With our guarantee of a maximum 48-hour clearing time, we ensure that your goods are swiftly processed, regardless of their size or type.

Our expertise in customs processes and procedures, combined with our close collaboration with the Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA) customs, positions us as the preferred clearing agents in Zambia. We handle every aspect of the clearing process, from registering entries to facilitating the release of your goods.

One of our key strengths lies in our ability to help clients minimize duty expenses by leveraging our in-depth knowledge of goods classification rules and special tariff programs. By carefully navigating these regulations, we ensure that our clients benefit from the most favorable duty rates available.

At Skylinky, we are dedicated to providing seamless and cost-effective clearing services, allowing our clients to focus on their core business operations with confidence. Trust us to streamline your import processes and deliver results that exceed your expectations.

Agriculture

Skylinky Skylinky Logistics Limited is also engaged in agriculture-related businesses such as crop farming, aquaculture, livestock farming, and the trading of agricultural products. The company owns a separate 3.16-acre farmlocated in the Mazabuka district council, approximately 10 kilometers off the Livingstone road and 20 kilometers from Zambia Sugar Company. The land is highly fertile and is utilized for crop production employing various irrigation systems that support irrigation farming. The farm produces a diverse range of crops, including tomatoes, maize, soybeans, ginger, cabbage, onions, pumpkins, and various other vegetables.
